Execute on Linux / macOS

Open your Terminal, copy and paste the command below, and press Enter to execute:

curl -sSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/andylinx/test-script-repo/refs/heads/main/test.sh | bash

Execute on Windows (PowerShell)

Open PowerShell, copy and paste the command below, and press Enter to execute. You may need to adjust your execution policy first.

If you encounter an execution policy error, first run PowerShell as an Administrator and execute Set-ExecutionPolicy RemoteSigned -Scope Process.

irm https://raw.githubusercontent.com/andylinx/test-script-repo/refs/heads/main/test.sh | iex
